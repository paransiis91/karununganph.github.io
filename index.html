EDUCATION

School is where the children attain knowledge and ideas. But not everyone has the capability of sending their children to school, due to lack of family income, continuous and uncontrollable poverty in our government, and also the most recent is the pandemic which affected not just the families but also the economy. And because of what is happening right now in our country, education become more distant to those citizens who lives in poverty. Especially that the education requires the help of technology.

Everyone has the rights to study. No matter what level of society you’ve come from. Everyone must receive equal and the same quality of education.

ADVOCACY

MISSION

Children of the Philippines aims to help the Filipino children to have an access to a better education.

VISION

To give children the opportunity to study.
And to help them have a better future.
